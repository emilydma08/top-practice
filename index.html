Homemade Boba Recipies

Passionfruit Boba

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Boba
  • Passionfruit Jam
  • Ice
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Heat water on stove until boiling, then add boba
  2. Boil for 3 minutes, then simmer for 3 more minutes
  3. Take boba off stove and drain
  4. In a cup, add boba as desired
  5. Add 3 spoonfuls of passionfruit jam
  6. Add ice and water to preference
  7. Mix well

Brown Sugar Milk Tea

Ingredients

  • Uncooked boba
  • Brown sugar
  • Milk tea powder
  • Water
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Heat water on stove until boiling, then add boba
  2. Boil for 3 minutes, then simmer for 3 more minutes
  3. Take boba off stove and drain, then add to a bowl
  4. Add 3 spoonfuls of brown sugar, mix until dissolved
  5. Dissolve milk tea powder in 1/4 cup of boiling water
  6. In a cup, add boba as desired
  7. Add ice then milk until only a little volume left
  8. Pour in concentrated milk tea at the top
  9. Mix well
